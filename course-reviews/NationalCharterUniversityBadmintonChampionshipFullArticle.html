National Charter University Badminton Championship

By: University of Bahrain
Published February 15, 2024
Category Sports

The University of Bahrain proudly hosted the first National Charter University Badminton Championship, showcasing exceptional student athletic talent from across the kingdom.

Championship Highlights

The tournament, held February 12-15, 2024 at UoB's Sports Complex, featured 32 players from 8 universities competing in singles and doubles matches. The event celebrated the spirit of the National Charter through friendly competition and sportsmanship.

"This championship embodies the values of unity and excellence in our National Charter,"

said Prof. Khalid Al-Baker, Director of Student Activities.

Key Results

  • UoB's Ali Hassan won gold in men's singles
  • Bahrain Polytechnic took women's doubles title
  • Record attendance of 500 spectators for finals
  • Special exhibition match with national team players

Future Plans

The success of this inaugural event has paved the way for:

  1. Annual inter-university badminton championship
  2. Expansion to include more sports disciplines
  3. Scholarship opportunities for outstanding athletes

The University of Bahrain continues to promote sports excellence as part of its holistic student development program, with plans already underway for next year's championship.
