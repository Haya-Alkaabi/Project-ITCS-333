UoB Hosts Astronauts for Satellite Launch

By: University of Bahrain
Published March 5, 2025
Category University Events

The University of Bahrain hosted Emirati astronauts to mark the historic launch of Bahrain's "Al Munther" satellite, strengthening space science collaboration in the region.

Event Highlights

The event, held on March 3-5, 2025, featured presentations by UAE astronauts about their space missions and the growing importance of space technology in the GCC region. The visit coincided with Bahrain's first satellite launch, marking a new era in the nation's space program.

"This collaboration bridges space science education between our nations,"

said Dr. Fatima Al-Jaber, UoB's Dean of Engineering.

Key Activities

  • Live viewing of the "Al Munther" satellite launch
  • Astronaut-led workshops for engineering students
  • Signing of space science cooperation agreement
  • Demonstration of satellite ground station at UoB

Future Collaborations

The event established several new initiatives:

  1. Joint UAE-Bahrain space research program
  2. Student exchange program for space technology studies
  3. Annual GCC Space Science Symposium to be hosted at UoB

The University of Bahrain continues to expand its STEM programs, with space technology becoming a key focus area following this landmark event.
