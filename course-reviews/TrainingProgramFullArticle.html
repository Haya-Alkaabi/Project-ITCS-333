UOB Shines in Sixth Arab Youth Leaders Training Program

By: University of Bahrain
Published March 16, 2025
Category University Achievements

The University of Bahrain demonstrated exceptional participation in the Sixth Training Program for Preparing Arab Youth Leaders, showcasing the institution's commitment to developing future leaders.

Program Overview

The training program, held in Cairo from March 10-16, 2025, brought together 120 participants from 22 Arab countries. UOB's delegation of 8 students and 2 faculty advisors stood out for their active engagement and innovative projects.

"Our students demonstrated remarkable leadership potential and cultural understanding throughout the program,"

said Dr. Ahmed Al-Mannai, faculty advisor and Director of Student Activities at UOB.

Key Achievements

  • Won first place in the intercultural communication workshop
  • Received special recognition for community development proposal
  • Selected to present at the closing ceremony
  • Established partnerships with 5 other Arab universities

Future Impact

The experience gained from this program will directly benefit UOB's leadership development initiatives, with plans to:

  1. Launch a new Arab Youth Leadership Club on campus
  2. Host a regional leadership conference in 2026
  3. Integrate program methodologies into student activities

The University of Bahrain continues to strengthen its position as a regional leader in student development and cross-cultural exchange programs.
