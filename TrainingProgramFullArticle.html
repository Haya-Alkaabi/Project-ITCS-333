UOB Wins Prestigious Green Campus Initiative Award

Edit
By: University of Bahrain Published: November 15, 2023

The University of Bahrain has been awarded the prestigious Green Campus Initiative award for its outstanding contributions to environmental sustainability through innovative campus projects.

This recognition comes after UOB successfully implemented a comprehensive solar energy program that has reduced the university's carbon footprint by 40% in the past year alone. The award was presented during the International Green Universities Summit held in Singapore.

Key Achievements

  • Installation of 5,000 solar panels across campus buildings
  • Water conservation systems saving 2 million gallons annually
  • Zero-waste initiatives in all cafeterias and food outlets
  • EV charging stations for electric vehicles

"This award reflects our commitment to sustainable development and environmental stewardship," said Dr. Mohammed Al-Ansari, UOB's Sustainability Officer. "We're proud to lead by example in Bahrain's higher education sector."
